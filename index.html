The Ultimate Guide To Cold Thermogenesis

"Cool" Health Benefits Of Cold Exposure.

Cold thermogenesis is the practice of intentionally exposing parts of the body to specific levels of cold stress. Solid research has shown that cold thermogenesis brings a host of health benefits, from increasing metabolism to helping to control blood glucose levels, reducing inflammation, improving sleep and recovery, and potentially fighting certain types of cancer, as well as promoting overall longevity.
